What is the warranty period for imnaaa headphones?
All imnaaa headphones come with a standard one-year warranty, covering manufacturing defects and performance issues. If you encounter any problems within this period, please contact our support team for assistance. We strive to ensure your satisfaction and will work to resolve any issues promptly.
How do I connect my headphones to my device?
To connect your imnaaa headphones, simply power them on and enable Bluetooth on your device. Search for available devices and select 'imnaaa Headphones' from the list. Once connected, you will hear a confirmation sound. For wired connections, plug the headphone jack into the audio port of your device.
Can I use my headphones while charging?
Yes, you can use your imnaaa headphones while they are charging. However, for optimal performance and battery longevity, we recommend allowing them to charge fully before use. This ensures you experience the best sound quality and functionality without interruptions.
